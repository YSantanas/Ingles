### Verbo "TO BE" - "SER O ESTAR"
I am   ***(Yo soy)***
You Are ***(Tú eres)***
He Is ***(él es)***
She Is ***(ella es)***
It Is ***(eso es)***

WE ARE (Nosotros somos)
THEY ARE (Ellos son)
